The Ritual.

A New Spirit for Your Sauna.

This is not just a sauna scent. It is an invitation to transform your ritual. We blended ancient Eastern wisdom with the timeless Finnish sauna tradition, creating a multi-sensory experience designed to ground your mind and relax your body.

How to Use Your Ritual

1. STEEP

Place one pouch in 3-5 liters (approx. 1 gallon) of hot water in your sauna bucket. Let it steep for 5-10 minutes.

2. ENJOY

Ladle the fragrant, infused water onto the hot sauna stones (kiuas). Breathe deep and enjoy the therapeutic steam (löyly).

3. PRO-TIP

Don't throw the pouch away! Place the hot, wet herbal pouch in a corner of the sauna room. It acts as a passive diffuser.

The Four Rituals

Forest Rain (Metsäsade).

It doesn’t just smell like a forest. It feels like one. This ritual is your escape from the noise. As the steam rises, it’s not just pine you smell, it’s the clean, crisp purity of a deep Nordic forest after a cleansing rain. It’s designed to quiet the static in your mind and help you breathe deeper. This is your reset button.

Fireside (Takkatuli).

This is more than warmth. This is comfort. We designed this ritual for the cold nights when your body feels tense and your mind is tired. The infused steam, rich with ginger and deep herbs, creates a penetrating warmth that cocoons your body, relaxes tight shoulders, and brings a profound sense of grounding. This is your sanctuary.

Summer Courtyard (Kesäpiha).

This isn’t just a scent. It’s a moment of peace. We captured the feeling of a quiet, sun-drenched garden. The soft, green, and gentle floral steam is designed to soothe, not overwhelm. It calms a busy mind and smooths the edges of a stressful day. This is your meditation.

Mountain Wind (Vuorituuli).

This isn’t just fresh. It’s alive. Designed for the moments you feel blocked or sluggish, this ritual is a jolt of pure energy. The powerful, uplifting steam cuts through the mental fog, awakens your senses, and makes you feel clear and renewed. This is your focus.
